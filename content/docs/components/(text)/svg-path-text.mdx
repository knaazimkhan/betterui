## Overview

The `SVGPathText` component allows you to animate text along a customizable SVG path. It creates a smooth, dynamic animation where the text follows a curved path, making it ideal for adding interactive or eye-catching typography to your web projects.

You can adjust the path, duration, and text styling to suit your needs.
